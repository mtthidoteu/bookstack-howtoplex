Downloading the Plex client

Plex is a media streaming software designed to be linked to an existing Plex Server. You will be using the [Name] server and its content. It has applications available for almost anything!

Attention! Be careful not to download the 'Plex Media Server' but the 'Plex Client'. If you are confused, please feel free to contact your referrer.

For Windows
  1. Goto https://www.plex.tv/en-gb/media-server-downloads/#plex-app

Setting Default Quality

Similar to any other streaming platform, Plex can provide different qualities. This guide will cover setting up the default quality, which can be changed at any time directly while playing content.


Setting Plex's default Playback Quality

  1. Open the Plex client on the relevant device
  2. Find the settings page
  3. In the setting page, find 'Quality' OR 'Remote Streaming Quality
  4. Set it to the desired quality (see below)

 

(Optional, if unknown) Determining Internet Speed

To determine your internet speed, we would recommend using: https://librespeed.org/

  1. Goto https://librespeed.org/
  2. Press 'Start'
  3. Wait for the test to finish
  4. Remember/Write down your 'Download Speed'

Understanding & Setting up the Plex App

By default, the Plex app will come with a lot of its own content. We don't recommend it and we will describe how to turn it off. 

Hiding the default Movies/TV Shows 

  1. On the Left you will see a list of Content Sources.
  2. Next to 'Movies & Shows on Plex', press the three dots and select 'Unpin'.
  3. Next to 'Live TV on Plex', press the three dots and select 'Unpin'.

Pinning the [NAME] Plex Server's Content

  1. Again, on the left menu, you will see a list of Content Sources.
  2. Expand the sources by clicking 'More'
  3. Under "[Name] PLEX', you will see 'Movies' and 'TV Shows'
  4. Next to 'Movies', use the three dots to select 'Pin'
  5. Next to 'TV Shows', use the three dots to select 'Pin'

That's it! Your Plex app has been setup and you are ready to watch content at its most optimal quality! Repeat for every device you wish to watch Plex from!
